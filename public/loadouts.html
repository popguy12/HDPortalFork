About the loadouts

Hideous Destructor has three zones for player encumbrance, which affect fatigue/stamina and sprinting capability (you can see which zone you are in by the color of your encumbrance number: green, yellow, or red). Accordingly, the list on this page was split into three categories, sorted by how much bulk each loadout starts the player with.

A lighter, dual-role variant of the Support Gunner loadout. SMG replaces the pistol for CQB encounters.
