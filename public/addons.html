About the addons

Most if not all people tend to play Hideous Destructor with at least one addon. Usually a sprite/sound replacement or a weapon. This page contains mostly HD addons, and a few non-HD addons that work well with the mod.

Disclaimer I: Some addons may be outdated. Hideous Destructor is updated constantly, so compatibility is not always guaranteed. If the mod does not work with a stable release, try the master branch.

Disclaimer II: Direct links to Discord downloads may become outdated over time. The links to the posts themselves, however, are always up-to-date, but you would need to be in the server to use those.

by Sledge et al.

The companion mod to my skinpack. These are "personal" weapons, mostly made for myself and for co-op friends. Unique Enemy Sounds

by Yholl

A mod for HD that makes every single sound an enemy makes unique! This means adding extra sounds to everything that shared sounds previously.

With this, you can hear the Hell Knight stalking behind the monster closet, hear the Jackboot getting ready to blast you, hear your grenade injure the Baron hiding behind a corner!

This also has compatibility with Melodica's VisualZombies mod, which adds unique sprites to all zombie and imp variants. This mod will add unique sounds to each of those variants if it is loaded afterwards.

AI Director

by Josh771
Does not require HD

A different approach to dynamic monster / item spawning, as compared to Demon Counterstrike. Attempts intelligent guesses about the player's state and actions when spawning enemies. Uses existing monster spawnpoints as reference nodes (instead of the player's past locations), and uses the level's existing monster composition for spawnlists (so if a map has lots of imps, then the director will use lots of imps). Simpler configuration options make it easier to tweak for desired difficulty and flow.

Demon CounterStrike

by Laggy
Does not require HD

Demon CounterStrike is a mod that simply spawns monsters and items around any place you have visited over time. You can customize how often things spawn, or how many seconds after you enter a level they will start spawning.

Immerse (HD Version)

by Josh771

It is a combination of Nash's Tilt++, Zombie Killer's footsteps, and a new version of my RealAim script combined with Thief-style leaning. Your camera will tilt and bob in harmony with the sound of your footfalls, all synchronized to bring a heightened illusion of actual concerted bipedal movement.

The best (and probably only) Kill Tally mod on the market! Count kills for every weapon and every monster! Track your progress with a XP system and fancy badges! Take screenshots of your hunting board! Get a stupidly huge amount of medals for your fists! The world is your oyster!
