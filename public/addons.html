About the addons

Most if not all people tend to play Hideous Destructor with at least one addon. Usually a sprite/sound replacement or a weapon. This page contains mostly HD addons, and a few non-HD addons that work well with the mod.

Disclaimer I: Some addons may be outdated. Hideous Destructor is updated constantly, so compatibility is not always guaranteed. If the mod does not work with a stable release, try the master branch.

Disclaimer II: Direct links to Discord downloads may become outdated over time. The links to the posts themselves, however, are always up-to-date, but you would need to be in the server to use those.
