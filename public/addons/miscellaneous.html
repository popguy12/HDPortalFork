AI Director

by Josh771
Does not require HD

A different approach to dynamic monster / item spawning, as compared to Demon Counterstrike. Attempts intelligent guesses about the player's state and actions when spawning enemies. Uses existing monster spawnpoints as reference nodes (instead of the player's past locations), and uses the level's existing monster composition for spawnlists (so if a map has lots of imps, then the director will use lots of imps). Simpler configuration options make it easier to tweak for desired difficulty and flow.

Demon CounterStrike

by Laggy
Does not require HD

Demon CounterStrike is a mod that simply spawns monsters and items around any place you have visited over time. You can customize how often things spawn, or how many seconds after you enter a level they will start spawning.

Immerse (HD Version)

by Josh771

It is a combination of Nash's Tilt++, Zombie Killer's footsteps, and a new version of my RealAim script combined with Thief-style leaning. Your camera will tilt and bob in harmony with the sound of your footfalls, all synchronized to bring a heightened illusion of actual concerted bipedal movement.
