Monster Skins/Sounds

Individual sprite, graphic, and sound replacements for monsters.

Compatible with multiplayer by design.

Last updated: 05/05/21

Advanced Dragon Guard

by Sirus

A skinpack replacing the chaingunner with a more intimidating variant with a helmet that doesn't do shit to stop bullets whatsoever, and new sounds.

Ashes Raider Zombies

by sonoda
Does not require HD

Enemy reskin mod that turn zombies into raiders from Ashes series.

Big Top Crew

by kris

Audio mod that replaces the sight, pain, action, and death sounds for the chaingunner and the revenant with sounds from thematically fitting enemies in CarnEvil. Compatible with other enemy audio mods as long as you load it before others, & is MP compatible.

Escape From Tarkov Zombiemen Voice Pack

by Potetobloke

May confuse and/or irritate actual Russian speakers.

Half-Life: Alyx Combine Voice Pack

by Potetobloke

Replaces the default zombiemen sounds.

Poteto's Enemy Sounds

by Potetobloke

Replaces the sounds of most enemies.

Terrorist Enemy Sprites

by Melodica

Terrorist sprites for zombies.

Unique Enemy Sounds

by Yholl

A mod for HD that makes every single sound an enemy makes unique! This means adding extra sounds to everything that shared sounds previously.

With this, you can hear the Hell Knight stalking behind the monster closet, hear the Jackboot getting ready to blast you, hear your grenade injure the Baron hiding behind a corner!

This also has compatibility with Melodica's VisualZombies mod, which adds unique sprites to all zombie and imp variants. This mod will add unique sounds to each of those variants if it is loaded afterwards.

Visual Zombies

by Melodica

Intends to give new sprites to a few enemies that don't have them, while also keeping the addon coop compatible (As in, one person can have it loaded while another one doesn't without causing a desync).

Visual Zombies Brightmaps

by Wanzer
Requires Visual Zombies

Adds simple brightmaps to Melodica's Visual Zombies mod.
