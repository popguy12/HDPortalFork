Monster Picker

by Williams

What was originally intended to be a quick little addon for myself called "Milsim Doom" which would replace every enemy with Undead Soldiers for a little more realism I thought - "this is pretty simple and i dont really have anything to do today" so I overcomplicated the fuck out of it and learned new things along the way.

I present to you Monster Picker. It allows you to replace all HDest monsters with different groups of monster types and it allows you to chose what monsters are not replaced. If you want more realism, replace all the monsters with Zombiemen. If you don't like the overpowered or tanky demons in your game but you want a hint of challenge and difficulty then pick and choose which heavy demons will spawn and which will not. You can customize whatever you want.

This should help de-slaughterify any slaughtermap of your choosing, since you can just replace everything with weaker enemies.

Replace Spawns - When turned off all the spawns should behave as the mapper intended.

Replace Type - What demons are replaced with.

X Spawning - When turned on the demon will not be replaced and will spawn as normal.
