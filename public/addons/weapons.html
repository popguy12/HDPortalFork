Peppergrinder

by Sledge, prettyFist, Eric
Community highlight
Confirmed multiplayer compatibility

The companion mod to my skinpack. These are "personal" weapons, mostly made for myself and for co-op friends. As such, they are mostly intended to be one-off customs, with spare ammo and extra guns not being found in the field (except for backpacks). Supports Accensus' HD Injector, in case you'd like to see these puppies in the wild rather than just in loadouts and backpacks.

Sawn-Off Slayer

by Tartlman a.k.a. bogus

Adds a sawn-off variant of the slayer. Can either be obtained occasionally in normal gameplay or started with, using the loadout code "ssg". The sawn off variant has more recoil and less damage, but it is also shorter and lighter.
